Eu existo

A esplanada é, a seguir à roda, a melhor invenção do homem.

Nov
13
burning in hell
this piece of me fits in nowhere
as other people find things
to do
with their time
places to go
with one another
things to say
to each other.
I am
burning in hell
some place north of Mexico.
flowers don’t grow here.

Charles Bukowski
