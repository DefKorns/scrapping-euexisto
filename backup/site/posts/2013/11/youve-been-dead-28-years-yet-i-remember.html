Eu existo

A esplanada é, a seguir à roda, a melhor invenção do homem.

Nov
05
you’ve been dead 
28 years 
yet I remember you 
better than any of 
the rest; 
you were the only one 
who understood 
the futility of the 
arrangement of 
life; 
all the others were only 
displeased with 
trivial segments, 
carped 
nonsensically about 
nonsense; 
Jane, you were 
killed by 
knowing too much. 
here’s a drink 
to your bones 
that 
this dog 
still 
dreams about.
