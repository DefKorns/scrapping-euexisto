Eu existo

A esplanada é, a seguir à roda, a melhor invenção do homem.

Oct
24

I am
a series of
small victories
and large defeats
and I am as
amazed
as any other
that
I have gotten
from there to
here
without committing murder
or being
murdered;
without
having ended up in the
madhouse.

CB
