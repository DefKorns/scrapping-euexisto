They were open. The Mexican girl who gave him his coffee looked at him as if he were a human being. The poor knew life. A good girl. Well, a good enough girl. They all meant trouble. Every­thing meant trouble. He remembered a statement he'd heard some­where: the Definition of Life is Trouble.

Charles Bukowski, South of No North
