In the band’s code, heroin was referred to as ‘Kentucky Fried Chicken’. Ryder is said to have walked out of a meeting with EMI in 1994, as the Mondays were about to sign a £1.7million contract, telling executives he was “going for a KFC” and would be back soon. He never returned and the deal fell through.
